Introduction

In is our desire to bring you a taste of Baltimore's finest dishes within our bistro. You will find culturally diverse dishes. We opened our doors in 1994 and shared our tastes ever since. Make reservations and experience this fine gift today.

Our goal is to expand worldwide and cater to all specialties. We have a selection of meals for everyone to enjoy. We only serve our freshest fruits and vegetables because our customers deserve nothing less but the best. Our responsibility is to keep you satiated.

Specialties
